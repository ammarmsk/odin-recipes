Lasagna

A delicous taco lasagna

The Real Lasagna Possibly one of the oldest pastas invented, lasagna was originated in the city of Naples, Italy.
Lasagna is a very popular and traditional dish that is served through families and generations over time

ingredients

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1/4-inch strips
  • ½ cup lime juice
  • 2 (1 ounce) packets taco seasoning mix (such as Old El Paso®)
  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup salsa
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 (8 ounce) package no-boil lasagna noodles, or to taste

Steps

  1. Combine chicken, lime juice, and taco seasoning in a large bowl.
    Stir to make sure chicken is evenly covered.
  2. cook chicken mixture over medium heat until firm,about 15 mins
  3. mix 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese , sour cream, and salsa in a bowl.Season with salt and pepper
  4. put a few spoonfuls of the sour cream mixture in the bottom of a 9x9-inch baking dish.
    layer noodles and checkin on top.
    cover with remaining 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  5. bake in the preaheated oven until noodles are tender and cheese is melted , about 1 hour
