Basted Eggs

Freshly made Eggs on Toast

Similar to sunny side up eggs, basted eggs are cracked directly on the frying pan. However, basted eggs have a slightly cooked yolk, instead of a runny yolk.
Therefore, when you eat and enjoy a basted egg, the egg yolk won't drip across the plate. Basted eggs are also a healthy alternative to fried eggs.

ingredients

  • 1 1/2 tablespoons salted butter
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon water
  • 1/8 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch flaky sea salt, or to taste

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a medium nonstick skillet over medium heat
  2. carefully crack eggs into the skillet and let cook until whites have almost set, about 1 minute
  3. Add water,cover skillet with a lid or cookie sheet , reduce heat to medium-low
  4. Remove lid and transfer eggs to a serving plate. Garnish with pepper and flaky salt
  5. Serve immedietly
